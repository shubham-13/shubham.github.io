All about Myself

The journey of my life began on 1st of Februrary 1996.T hat was the day I was blessed upon my parents and after a while was given the name Shubham Singhal. Well my life's been great ever since the day (ofcourse excepts some bumps that everyone gets). Being the elder brother, I faced nothing such as torture of an elder sibling rather I was the one doing it.

Well I had almost everything one could wish for. A great family, a nice and comfortable life and the best friends.Because we were tenants initially, we had to move a lot. And still every place I went, I somehow managed to find some great people who till day are my best friends.

I am not an extrovert. I prefer to live in my own world. I spent most of my childhood time either studying or playing Cricket.I was always the topper of of my class so studies was not much of a problem. And it was just me who had a real cricket bat, it was me who always batted first. Those were really great times.

The three most important things in my life are my family, my friends and my passion.Though I have not yet found out the one true passion of mine but still what I do i always try to give my best on it. And I will go to any heights or depths to do whatever I can for these three things.

Well that was all about myself in a nut-shell !
